Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and the 11th President of India. He is known as the "Missile Man of India" for his contribution to India's space and missile programs.

He played a key role in India's nuclear tests and inspired millions of students with his vision and leadership.
